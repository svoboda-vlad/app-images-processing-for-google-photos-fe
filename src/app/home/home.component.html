Images processing for Google Photos allows you to:
  • reduce images size - resize your images using @digitalascetic/ngx-pica package (in order to save storage space for Google Photos)
  • group images to albums - group your images based on date and time into albums with predefined name
  • preview images in albums - view images by album one by one with larger previews
  • upload albums and images to Google Photos - create new albums on Google Photos and upload your images to the albums
